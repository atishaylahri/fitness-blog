## About Gym Stack

Gym Stack is a fitness equipment and gear review blog dedicated to helping you make smarter buying decisions for your training journey.

Whether you're setting up a home gym, upgrading your workout gear, or looking for the best recovery tools, we cut through the noise to give you honest, research-backed recommendations.

### What We Cover

- **Home gym equipment** — dumbbells, barbells, racks, benches, cardio machines
- **Fitness wearables** — trackers, smartwatches, heart rate monitors
- **Recovery gear** — massage guns, foam rollers, compression sleeves
- **Workout accessories** — bags, shoes, gloves, headphones, water bottles
- **Supplements** — protein, creatine, pre-workout, and more

### Our Approach

Every review is written with a focus on real-world performance, value for money, and who each product is actually suited for — whether you're a beginner just starting out or an experienced athlete optimising every detail.

We test, research, and compare so you don't have to.
